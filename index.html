Correlation Between League of Legends (LoL) Gaming Habits and Player Rank

Authors:

In the fall of 2015, one of our project's authors conducted a public survey exploring League of Legends' gamers' perceptions about toxic behavior in the game. Over 400 gamers responded. We visualized these gamers' rank in different game types within LoL to examine correlation.
